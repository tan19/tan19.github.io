PRML

  • The main focus of this book is Bayesian Machine Learning (chapters 5, 6, and 7 can be skipped).

  • The framework is graphical models (see Jordan2015 and WJ2008).

  • The inference algorithms are MCMC (see Liu1999, Geyer2011, and RC2011) and variational methods (Beal2003).

  • Kernel methods (see STC2004 and neural networks (see GBC2016) are briefly covered too.

  • See RW2006 for Gaussian processes, and Orbanz2014 for Bayesian Nonparametrics.

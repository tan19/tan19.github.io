>> Analysis

  • Calculus (7th ed.), by James Stewart.

  • Apostol, Tom. Mathematical Analysis (2nd ed.). Pearson, 1974.

  • Strichartz, Robert. The Way of Analysis (rev. ed.). Jones & Bartlett Learning, 2000.

  • Richard L. Wheeden. Measure and Integral.

  • Folland. Real Analysis: Modern Techniques and Their Applications

  • Torchinsky, Alberto. Real Variables. Westview Press, 1995.

  • Royden, Halsey & Fitzpatrick, Patrick. Real Analysis (4th ed.). Pearson, 2010.

  • Kreyszig, Erwin. Introductory Functional Analysis with Applications. Wiley, 1989.

  • Analysis Series by Walter Rudin

    • Principles of Mathematical Analysis, by Walter Rudin

    • Real and Complex Analysis, by Walter Rudin

    • Functional Analysis, by Walter Rudin

  • Analysis Series by Elias M. Stein and Rami Shakarchi

    • Fourier Analysis: An Introduction, by Elias M. Stein and Rami Shakarchi

    • Complex Analysis, by Elias M. Stein and Rami Shakarchi

    • Real Analysis: Measure Theory, Integration, and Hilbert Spaces, by Elias M. Stein and Rami Shakarchi

    • Functional Analysis: Introduction to Further Topics in Analysis, by Elias M. Stein and Rami Shakarchi

  • Hubbard and Hubbard. Vector Calculus, Linear Algebra, and Differential Forms.

  • Sternberg, Loomis. Advanced Calculus.

  • Tristan Needham. Visual Complex Analysis.

>> Algebra

  • Linear Algebra and Its Applications, by Gilbert Strang

  • Linear Algebra Done Right, by Sheldon Axler

  • Linear Algebra, by Kenneth M Hoffman and Ray Kunze

  • Linear Algebra, by Stephen H. Friedberg, Arnold J. Insel, Lawrence E. Spence

  • Advanced Linear Algebra, by Steven Roman

  • Matrix Analysis, by Roger A. Horn and Charles R. Johnson

  • Applied Numerical Linear Algebra, by James W. Demmel

  • Matrix Computations, by Gene H. Golub and Charles F. van Van Loan

  • Iterative Methods for Sparse Linear Systems, by Yousef Saad

  • Algebra, by Michael Artin.

  • Abstract Algebra, by David S. Dummit and Richard M. Foote

  • Seber, George. A Matrix Handbook for Statisticians. Wiley, 2007.

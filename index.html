My Intellectual curiosity lies in both Sciences and Humanities – Quantitative Finance and Cultural Anthropology, in particular.

For Quantitative Finance, my endeavor has been to build a pyramid: Finance raises questions, Statistics & Mathematics & Physics provide with models, Machine Learning & Algorithms & Optimization propose solutions, and Computer Science focuses on efficient implementation.

For Cultural Anthropology, I am interested in understanding the impact of {Politics, History, Philosophy} and {Music, Painting, Languages} on human activities.

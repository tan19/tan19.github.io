Welcome to My Homepage

I received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China, in 2007; the M.Phil degree in Computer Science from University of Cambridge, England, in 2008; and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science specializing in Machine Learning with double minors in Mathematics and Statistics from Purdue University, in 2018. I work in the Data Science and Finance industries.

My intellectual curiosity lies in the problems, models, algorithms, and implementations of Machine Learning and Quantitative Finance, which center around the following two famous equations:

\[ \begin{array}{lll} {\text{Bayes’ Theorem }}&:& p(\theta|D) = \frac{\pi(\theta)p(D|\theta)}{\int_\Theta \pi(\theta)p(D|\theta) d\theta}\\ {\text{Ito's Lemma }}&:& df(t,X_t) = \left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial t} + \mu_t \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} + \frac{\sigma_t^2}{2}\frac{\partial^2f}{\partial x^2}\right)dt+ \sigma_t \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\,dB_t \end{array} \]

Parallel to the scientific world, I am interested in learning foreign languages (e.g., French, Japanese, and German), playing piano, and reading Chinese classics.

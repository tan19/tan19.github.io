英语文学史 ==

English Renaissance: 1500–1660

  • Elizabethan period (1558–1603)

  • Jacobean period: 1603–25

  • Late Renaissance: 1625–1660 Restoration Age: 1660–1700 18th century

  • Augustan literature (1700–1750)

  • Age of sensibility: 1750–1798 Romanticism (1798–1837) Victorian literature (1837–1901) Twentieth-century English literature

  • Modernism: 1901–1922

  • After modernism: 1940 to 2000

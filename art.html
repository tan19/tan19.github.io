== 西方美术史 ==
Italian Renaissance: Late 14th century to Early 16th century
Northern Renaissance: 16th century

Mannerism: 16th century
Baroque（巴洛克）: early 17th century to mid-early 18th century
Rococo（洛可可）: Mid-18th century

Neoclassicism（新古典主义）: mid-early 18th century to early 19th century
Romanticism（浪漫主义）: late 18th century to mid-19th century
Realism（现实主义）: 19th century
Impressionism（印象派）: 19th century

Modernism（现代派）: First half of the 20th century


Contemporary art: since second half of the 20th century

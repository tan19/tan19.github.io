Q: What's the difference between equity research and private equity?

A: Equity Research is about finding the valuation of the listed companies on stock exchanges while Private equity is researching and analyzing the private companies and interpreting your results.

Q: What is alpha and what is beta?

A: Investors call skill-driven stock-picking returns “alpha” and passive market exposure “beta”.

1 Markov Chains
A stochastic process can be dened quite generally as any collection of random
variables X(t), t T , dened on a common probability space, where T is a
subset of (, ) and is thought of as the time parameter set. If the random
variables X(t) all take on values from the xed set S , then S is called the state
space of the process.

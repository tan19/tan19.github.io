### Good Readings ###
>> Probability
- A First Course in Probability, by Sheldon Ross
- Stochastic Processes, by Sheldon Ross
- Introduction to Stochastic Processes, by Paul Gerhard Hoel, Sidney C. Port and Charles J. Stone
- An Introduction to Probability Theory and Its Applications, by William Feller

>> Probability Measure Theory
- A First Look at Rigorous Probability Theory, by Jeffrey S. Rosenthal
- A Course in Probability Theory, by Kai Lai Chung
- Probability and Measure, by Patrick Billingsley
- Probability: Theory and Examples, by Rick Durrett
- Foundations of Modern Probability, by Olav Kallenberg

>> Linear and Generalized Linear Models
- Applied Linear Statistical Models, by Michael H. Kutner, John Neter, Christopher J. Nachtsheim, William Li
- Generalized Linear Models, by P. McCullagh and John A. Nelder
- Linear Regression Analysis, by George A. F. Seber and Alan J. Lee

>> Mathematical Statistics
- Statistical Inference, by George Casella and Roger L. Berger
- Kendall's Advanced Theory of Statistics, by Alan Stuart, Keith Ord and Steven Arnold
- The Foundations of Statistics, by Leonard J. Savage
- Theory of Statistics, by Mark J. Schervish
